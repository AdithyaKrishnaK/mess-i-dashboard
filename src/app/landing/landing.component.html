Welcome To Mess-I :)

For Rebate Application

Navigate to the Rebate section to:

  • View all rebate applications and their status
  • Submit a new application

Please submit the application at least 2 days before the rebate begins.

Rebates can be requested for durations between 3 and 30 days.

For Guest-Entry Application

Navigate to the Guest-Entry section to:

  • View all guest entry applications with statuses and token numbers
  • Submit a new guest entry application

You can withdraw your application until the mess starting time for the respective meal.

Guest entries can be made for a maximum of 3 days in advance.

Guest-Entry Timings:

  • For H10:
  • Lunch: 12:00pm - 12:45pm & 2:00pm - 2:30pm
  • Dinner: 7:30pm - 8:30pm
