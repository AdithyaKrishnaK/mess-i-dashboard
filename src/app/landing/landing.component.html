Welcome To Mess-I :)

For Rebate application

Navigate to the Rebate section

You will be able to see all your rebate applications and their status

To submit an application click on New Request and fill out the details

To ensure that there is sufficient time for approval, please submit the application at least 2 days before the duration of rebate begins

You can only submit rebate for a duration more than 3 days and less than 30 days

For Guest-Entry application

Navigate to the guest-entry section

Here you will be able to see all your guest entry applications with their statuses and your token number

To make a new application, click on new request and fill in the appropriate details. You will see the current meal availability count

You will be able to withdraw your application till the mess starting time for the respective meal

Guest entries can only be made for a maximum of 3 days in advance
