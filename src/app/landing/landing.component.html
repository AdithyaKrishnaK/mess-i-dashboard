For Rebate application

Navigate to the Rebate section

You will be able to see all your rebate applications and their status

To submit an application click on New Request and fill out the details

To ensure that there is sufficient time for approval, please submit the application at least 2 days before the duration of rebate begins

You can only submit rebate for a duration more than 3 days and less than 30 days
